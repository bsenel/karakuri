# Karakuri Human Augmentation License Addendum (HALA)

Version 1.0

## Philosophy

Karakuri exists to augment human capability, improve
organizational effectiveness, and eliminate repetitive work.

Karakuri is intended to empower employees, not replace them.

Inspired by the philosophy of karakuri ningyō —
mechanisms designed to assist humans — this project
promotes human-centered automation.

---

## Permitted Usage

Organizations may freely use Karakuri for:

- Employee productivity improvement
- Workflow orchestration
- Decision support
- Research acceleration
- Knowledge management
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Reduction of repetitive/manual work
- Human-in-the-loop automation
- Training and upskilling employees

---

## Restricted Usage

Without a separate written agreement from the maintainers,
Karakuri may not be used where the primary intent or material
effect is workforce replacement.

Restricted activities include:

- Replacing employees performing substantially similar duties
through Karakuri automation
- Workforce reduction initiatives substantially enabled by
Karakuri
- Department replacement strategies where human oversight
is intentionally minimized
- Fully autonomous organizational operation intended to
remove equivalent human positions

---

## Human Augmentation Principle

Organizations using Karakuri should prioritize:

1. Employee augmentation over replacement
2. Human oversight for consequential decisions
3. Workforce enablement and reskilling
4. Responsible deployment of autonomous systems

---

## Enforcement

The maintainers reserve the right to revoke usage permissions
under this addendum for organizations acting in material
violation of these principles.

Organizations uncertain about compliance should contact
the maintainers.
